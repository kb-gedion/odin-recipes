Simple Pan Sauce

Elevate your chicken breast dinner with this simple and elegant Chicken Pan Sauce. Made with simple ingredients you already have in your kitchen, this sauce takes just 20 minutes to make and will please even the pickiest palates! Itâ€™s flavorful, juicy, and a must-have in your recipe book.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • Splash of Olive Oil
  • Seasoning for Chicken
  • 1/2 Shallot
  • 3 Cloves Garlic
  • 1/3 cup white wine
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3-5 cubes of butter
  • bunch of chives

Steps

  1. Season and cook chicken breast in a stainless steep pan however you want
  2. Once done, set aside, turn heat to medium low and add the shallots and garlic
  3. Once toasty and fragrant, add a healthy splash of white wine and let that reduce
  4. Once reduced, add the chicken stock and do the same
  5. Add cubes of cold butter slowly to emulsiphy and thicken up the sauce
  6. Once at the desired consistency, add the chives, kill the heat, squeeze some lemon juice, and season to your liking.
